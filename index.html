ABOUT US

Manufacturing excellence from Guangdong to the world

Located in Foshan, the heart of China's manufacturing hub, our factory spans over 50,000 square meters with advanced CNC machining centers, cold forging lines, and automated sorting systems. We produce over 2 billion fasteners annually—from standard screws to custom-engineered solutions for automotive, construction, and electronics industries.

Our commitment to precision means every batch undergoes rigorous quality control. ISO 9001 certified. Trusted by partners in 30+ countries.
